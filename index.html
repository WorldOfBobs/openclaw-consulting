FAQ

Common questions about OpenClaw setup and what to expect.

What exactly is OpenClaw and why do I need help installing it?
OpenClaw is a self-hosted AI assistant platform that runs on your own hardware and connects to AI providers like Claude. While the software is powerful, configuring it correctly ‚Äî keys, routing, integrations, security hardening ‚Äî takes time and expertise. We handle all of that so you start productive on day one instead of troubleshooting for weeks.
How long does an OpenClaw installation take?
A standard remote setup takes 1 to 3 business days from first contact to go-live. In-person engagements are typically completed within a single day on-site. We'll give you a specific timeline before we start.
What hardware do I need for OpenClaw?
Hardware requirements depend on your use case. A Mac mini is a popular choice for home and small business deployments because of its efficiency, reliability, and macOS compatibility. We will advise on the right specs for your workload during the intake process. Hardware is not included in our packages and is purchased separately.
Do I need any technical background to use OpenClaw after setup?
No. Our setup packages include documentation and a walkthrough so you can use OpenClaw confidently without any technical background. The Premium and In-Person tiers include extended support time specifically for this.
What are the ongoing costs after setup?
Our fee is one-time. After setup, your only recurring costs are AI provider charges (typically $100 to $200 per month depending on usage and model) and any hardware or hosting costs you choose. There are no subscriptions or retainer fees unless you opt into additional support.
Can you set up OpenClaw for a team or business?
Yes. Our Premium Remote and In-Person packages are designed for business deployments and include automation consulting and workflow integration. Contact us to discuss your team size and use case.
Do you offer support after the setup is complete?
All packages include a follow-up support window after go-live. The Remote Setup includes 48 hours of follow-up. Premium and In-Person packages include an extended period. We can discuss ongoing support arrangements for larger deployments.
